About the Author

Reinn, also known by her pen name Secretlychasing, is an exclusive writer of Summit Media Pop Fiction since 2019. She has been writing Wattpad stories since 2013 and has published three books under Pop Fiction. Currently working as a medical technologist at a private hospital in Cavite, Philippines, Reinn was able to achieve her academic goals despite of some financial difficulties. She is passionate about writing stories and talking with her readers through her social media accounts. Besides writing, she is also fond of reading novels, watching K-dramas, hanging out with her friends and family, and playing with her pet named Lexc. She dreams of becoming a bestselling author.
